Aid Stations

This is a cup-less race. Bring your own hydration device to carry and consume fluids. Water and Skratch sports drink will be available at all aid stations in addition to peanut butter and jelly sandwiches, potato chips, M&M's, fig newton cookies, coke, sprite, basic first aid and more. There will also be a limited number of Honey Stinger gels available.
