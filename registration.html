Registration

Registration is now open! Visit https://www.edsregistration.com/events/80 to register.

All members of North Texas Trail Runners are elligible to receive a $15 discount through February 15, 2016. Membership must be current at the time of registration for this benefit. A special registration code will be e-mailed to current NTTR members. Visit www.nttr.org/join/ to join.

New/Renewal members, please forward a copy of your email confirmation from active.com to grasslandstrailrun@gmail.com and we will provide you with a discount code.

Refunds

Refunds will be allowed until February 15, 2016. All refunds will be subject to a $10 adminstrative charge. Registration transaction fees are non-refundable.

Race Changes

Race changes will be allowed prior to March 16, 2016 if space allows. Switching to a longer distance race will require the payment of the difference in registration cost. No refunds will be provided for switching to a shorter distance.

Transfers

Transfers to another participant are allowed. After February 15, 2016 we cannot guarantee shirt sizes.
