Course/Aid station info This is a cup-less race. Please bring your own hydration device to carry and consume fluids. Water will be available at all aid stations and endurance drink will be available at the TADRA aid station. Aid stations will also offer peanut butter & jelly sandwiches, potato chips, m&m's, fig newtons, coke, sprite, basic first aid and more. There will also be a limited number of gels available. For frequency of aid stations please see the course info and maps portion of our site.

The trail is a cloverleaf configuration with the start/finish located in the center.
Please see the Course Info/Maps area for more detailed race routes.
